Course Description.

Digital design is taking over the world. What does this mean for the products, experiences, and ideas that emerge in the digital landscape? How can we design them to communicate more clearly, improve quality of life, and empower people? More than ever, we need UX and UI designers who can navigate complex problems with humans at the center. In this course we will take a deep dive into UX and UI while exploring through design fundamentals. You will develop your own UX/UI project, applying visual design ideas like color selection and typography; design thinking elements like user research, ideation, and prototyping, among others. Guest industry experts will share career highlights and give feedback on student projects.

Learning Outcomes.

Understand the fundamentals of what makes a good design good.

Explore the UX Design lifecycle.

Differentiate between UI and UX Design.

Understand the different aspects of UI.

Create high fidelity interfaces using adobe Xd or Sketch.
